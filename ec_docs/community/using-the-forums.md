# Using the Forums

The forums on Extra Chill Community are where members discuss music, share experiences, and connect with fellow music enthusiasts. You can browse existing discussions, start new topics, and engage with the community through replies and upvotes.

## Browsing Forums

1. Visit the [homepage](https://community.extrachill.com/) to see available forums.
2. Forums include Local Scenes for discussing local music scenes and Music Discussion for general music topics.
3. Click on a forum to view its topics.
4. Use the search function to find specific topics.

## Creating a New Topic

1. From the homepage, click the "New Topic" button.
2. Fill in the topic title and content using the rich text editor.
3. You can upload images directly in the editor.
4. Select appropriate tags if available.
5. Click "Submit" to post your topic.

## Replying to Topics

1. Open a topic you want to reply to.
2. Scroll to the bottom and use the reply form.
3. Use the editor to format your response.
4. Mention other users by typing @username.
5. Submit your reply.

## Upvoting Content

You can upvote topics and replies you find helpful or interesting. Upvotes help highlight quality content and contribute to user ranks.

## Notifications

Stay updated with notifications for replies to your topics, mentions, and other activities.

## User Badges and Ranks

Members earn ranks based on their activity, and special badges indicate artists or team members.
