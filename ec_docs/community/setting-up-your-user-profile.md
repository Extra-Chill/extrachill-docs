# Setting Up Your User Profile

Personalize your profile to connect with fellow music enthusiasts and showcase your interests in the Extra Chill community.

## Accessing Your Profile Settings

Visit your profile edit page to customize your information. Your profile displays your activity across the platform, including forum posts and articles from the main site.

## Customizing Your Profile

### Avatar
Upload a profile picture to represent yourself in the community. This image appears next to your posts and in your profile.

### Custom Title
Choose a title that reflects your role or personality in the community. If left blank, it defaults to "Extra Chillian".

### Bio
Write a short description about yourself, your music interests, or your background.

### Local Scene
Enter your city or region to connect with people in your area.

### Your Links
Add links to your website, social media profiles, or music streaming platforms. Supported platforms include website, Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, YouTube, TikTok, Spotify, SoundCloud, Bandcamp, and GitHub. You can add multiple links and reorder them as needed.

## Managing Artist Profiles

If you have artist status, you can manage your artist profiles and link pages from your profile settings.

## Viewing Your Profile

After saving changes, view your updated profile to see how it appears to others.

Your profile helps others discover your contributions and connect with you across the Extra Chill platform.

For help with your profile or account settings, contact us at https://extrachill.com/contact/ or visit the tech support forum at https://community.extrachill.com/r/tech-support.
