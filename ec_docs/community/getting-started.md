# Getting Started in the Extra Chill Community

The Extra Chill Community is a music-focused forum where fans, artists, and industry professionals connect through meaningful discussions, music sharing, and social features. Whether you're here to discuss your favorite bands, get feedback on your music, or network with like-minded people, this guide will help you get started.

## Why Join the Community?

The community offers a unique space for genuine music connections that goes beyond typical social media. Key benefits include:

- **In-depth music discussions** in dedicated forums like Local Scenes and Music Discussion
- **Music feedback** where artists can share work and receive constructive input
- **Social features** like following users, upvoting content, and notifications
- **Cross-platform integration** with the main Extra Chill blog for seamless upvoting

## Creating Your Account

### Option 1: Email Registration
1. Visit [community.extrachill.com](https://community.extrachill.com)
2. Click "Register" in the top navigation or visit the [registration page](https://community.extrachill.com/login/#tab-register)
3. Provide your email address and create a password
4. Check your email for a verification link and click it to activate your account

### Option 2: Google Sign-In
1. Visit [community.extrachill.com](https://community.extrachill.com)
2. Click "Sign in with Google" on the login page
3. Choose your Google account and grant permission
4. If you have an existing Extra Chill account with the same email, you'll be automatically signed in
5. If this is your first time, a new account will be created automatically

Once registered, you'll be automatically logged in across all Extra Chill platforms (extrachill.com, artist.extrachill.com, etc.) thanks to the multisite authentication system.

## Understanding the Layout

After logging in, you'll see:

- **Navigation menu** with links to Recent activity, forum categories, and your profile
- **Homepage** showing featured forums, latest activity, and community highlights
- **User avatar menu** (top right) for accessing settings, notifications, and logout
- **Online users indicator** showing current community activity

## Exploring Forums

The community features several discussion areas:

- **Local Scenes** - Discuss music scenes in your area
- **Music Discussion** - General music topics and conversations
- **Extra Chill Articles** - Blog content discussions with enhanced commenting

Browse existing topics by clicking on forum names, or use the search function to find specific discussions.

## Participating in Discussions

### Starting a New Topic
1. Navigate to the forum where you want to post
2. Click the "New Topic" button
3. Enter a clear, descriptive title
4. Write your post using the rich text editor (supports images, links, and formatting)
5. Click "Submit" to publish

### Replying to Posts
1. Open any topic you're interested in
2. Scroll to the bottom or click "Reply" next to specific posts
3. Use the rich text editor to compose your response
4. Mention users with @username for notifications
5. Submit your reply

### Content Guidelines
- **Be kind** - Keep discussions respectful and constructive
- **Avoid spam** - Self-promotion is limited to designated areas
- **Stay on topic** - Keep political discussions minimal and civil
- **Respect copyright** - Only share content you have rights to
- **Protect privacy** - Remember all posts are public

## Using Social Features

### Upvoting Content
Click the upvote button (▲) on posts and replies you find valuable. Upvoted content helps highlight quality discussions and contributes to users' reputation scores.

### Following Users
Visit any user's profile and click "Follow" to add their posts to your personal feed. This is especially useful for keeping up with artists and industry professionals.

### Notifications
Check the bell icon in the top navigation for:
- Replies to your posts
- Mentions (@username)
- New followers
- Activity from users you follow

## Building Your Profile

### Basic Customization
1. Click your avatar in the top right and select "Settings"
2. Update your profile information, including:
   - Display name
   - Social media links
   - Personal bio
   - Custom title (defaults to "Extra Chillian")

### Avatar Upload
In your settings, you can upload a custom profile picture that will appear across all Extra Chill platforms.

### Artist Verification
If you're a musician or industry professional:
1. Go to your Settings page
2. Request verification to add a badge to your profile
3. Once approved, you'll get a special section to showcase your work

## Understanding the Rank System

Your community rank is based on points earned through activity:
- Creating topics and replies (2 points each)
- Receiving upvotes on your content (0.5 points each)
- Contributing to the main blog (10 points per post)

As you accumulate points, you'll progress through ranks from "Dew" to "Frozen Deep Space," displayed as badges next to your name.

## Key Tips for New Members

- **Introduce yourself** - Post in an appropriate forum to meet other community members
- **Read existing discussions** before starting new topics to avoid duplicates
- **Engage respectfully** - The community values constructive dialogue
- **Explore cross-platform features** - Your community account unlocks additional features on the main blog
- **Stay active** - Regular participation helps you build connections and rank

The Extra Chill Community is designed to foster genuine connections in the music world. Take your time exploring, and remember that every member—from casual fans to industry veterans—helps make this community special.
