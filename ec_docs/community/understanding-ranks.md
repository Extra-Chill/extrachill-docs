# Understanding Community Ranks

Your rank reflects your participation level in the Extra Chill Community. Earn points through forum activity and watch your rank climb from "Dew" all the way to "Frozen Deep Space."

## How You Earn Points

- **Topics**: 2 points each
- **Replies**: 2 points each
- **Upvotes received**: 0.5 points each
- **Blog posts published**: 10 points each (for contributors to the main Extra Chill site)

## Where to See Your Rank

- **Your profile page**: Shows your current rank and total points
- **Forum replies**: Your rank and points appear in the sidebar next to your posts
- **Leaderboard**: See how you compare to other community members

## Point Timing

- **Topics and replies**: Points update hourly
- **Upvotes**: Points update immediately when you receive them

There may be a brief delay before new points appear across all areas of the site.

## Forum Badges

You may notice special badges next to usernames in the forum:

- **Artist**: Verified musician or performer
- **Music Industry Professional**: Works in the music industry
- **Extra Chill Team Member**: Official staff member

These badges are separate from ranks and indicate verified roles within the community.
