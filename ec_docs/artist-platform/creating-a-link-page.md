# How to Create and Access Your Link Page

Welcome to the Extra Chill Artist Platform! A link page is your personal landing page where fans can discover all your music, social media, and other important links in one convenient place. It's like a digital business card that showcases your artistic presence.

## Getting Started: Join the Platform

To create your link page, you first need to join the Extra Chill community. Here's how:

1. **Visit the Join Page**: Go to [extrachill.link/join](https://extrachill.link/join) in your web browser.

2. **Account Check**: You'll be asked if you already have an Extra Chill Community account.
   - If you have an existing account, click "Yes, I have an account" and log in with your credentials.
   - If you're new to Extra Chill, click "No, I need to create an account" and fill out the registration form.

3. **Complete Registration**: Provide your email address, create a username, and set a password. Once registered, your artist profile and link page are automatically created for you!

4. **Welcome Setup**: After registration, you'll be guided to customize your new link page.

## Accessing Your Link Page

Your link page is instantly live and accessible to anyone with the URL. Here's how to find and share it:

1. **Your Unique URL**: Your link page lives at `extrachill.link/your-artist-name` (where "your-artist-name" is based on your artist profile name).

2. **Share It Everywhere**: Add this link to your social media bios, email signatures, business cards, or anywhere you want fans to find your music and content.

3. **Public Access**: Anyone can visit your link page without needing an account - it's designed for maximum discoverability.

## Managing Your Link Page

To customize and update your link page content:

1. **Log In**: Make sure you're logged into your Extra Chill account.

2. **Visit Management**: Go to [extrachill.link/manage-link-page](https://extrachill.link/manage-link-page).

3. **Select Your Artist**: If you have multiple artist profiles, choose which one you want to manage.

4. **Customize Away**: Use the intuitive editor to add your music links, social media profiles, bio, photos, and more. Your changes appear instantly in the live preview.

## Tips for a Great Link Page

- **Keep It Current**: Regularly update your links as you release new music or change social platforms.
- **Add Your Best Content**: Include your most important streaming platforms, social media, and any special offers for fans.
- **Personal Touch**: Add a photo and bio to make it feel authentic and connect with your audience.
- **Mobile Friendly**: Your link page looks great on phones, so fans can easily tap your links on the go.
