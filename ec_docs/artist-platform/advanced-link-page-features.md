# How to Use Advanced Link Page Features

## Overview
Take your link page to the next level with powerful features like analytics tracking, custom links, streaming integrations, and social sharing. These advanced tools help you connect better with your audience and grow your music career.

## Viewing Your Link Page Analytics

### Check Page Views and Link Clicks
1. Go to your link page management area
2. Click on the "Analytics" tab
3. View your page view trends in the interactive chart
4. See which links are getting the most clicks
5. Choose different date ranges to analyze performance over time

### Export Your Analytics Data
1. In the Analytics tab, click the "Export" button
2. Choose your preferred date range
3. Download your analytics as a CSV file to analyze in spreadsheet software

**Tip**: Check your analytics weekly to see which content resonates most with your fans and adjust your links accordingly.

## Adding Custom Links

### Create a Custom Link
1. In your link page editor, go to the "Links" section
2. Click "Add New Link"
3. Choose "Custom Link" from the platform options
4. Enter your website URL (like your official site or merch store)
5. Add a custom label (like "Official Website" or "Buy Merch")
6. Save your changes

### Reorder Your Links
1. In the Links section, click and drag any link to move it up or down
2. Release to drop it in the new position
3. Your changes save automatically and update the live preview

**Tip**: Put your most important links (like new music releases) at the top where fans will see them first.

## Integrating Streaming Platforms

### Add YouTube Videos
1. Go to the "Links" section of your link page editor
2. Click "Add New Link"
3. Paste your YouTube video URL
4. The system will automatically detect it as a YouTube link
5. Add a title and description for the video
6. Save to see the embedded video player on your link page

### Connect Music Streaming Services
1. In the "Social" section, click "Add Social Link"
2. Choose your streaming platform (Spotify, Apple Music, Bandcamp, etc.)
3. Paste your artist profile URL from that platform
4. The system will validate the URL and add the correct icon
5. Arrange the order to feature your most popular platforms first

**Tip**: YouTube integration works great for music videos, lyric videos, or behind-the-scenes content that plays directly on your link page.

## Managing Social Sharing

### Add Social Media Links
1. Navigate to the "Social" tab in your link page editor
2. Click "Add Social Link"
3. Choose from popular platforms like Instagram, Twitter, TikTok, Facebook, and more
4. Paste your profile URL
5. The system automatically adds the correct icon and validates the link

### Create Custom Social Links
1. In the Social section, select "Custom Link"
2. Enter any website URL you want to feature
3. Add a custom label like "Official Website" or "Join Mailing List"
4. Choose a custom icon or use the default link icon

**Tip**: Include links to your social platforms, email newsletter, and any other important websites. Fans can easily share your link page using the built-in share button.

## Using Premium Features

### Set Link Expiration
1. Go to the "Advanced" tab
2. Enable link expiration
3. Set the date and time when you want the link to expire
4. Choose what happens when it expires (hide the page, show a message, or redirect)
5. Add a custom expiration message if desired

### Create QR Codes
1. In the Advanced tab, click "Generate QR Code"
2. Your link page URL is automatically converted to a QR code
3. Download the QR code image to use on posters, stickers, or social media
4. Share the downloadable link with fans

### Set Up Email Subscriptions
1. In the Advanced tab, go to Subscription Settings
2. Choose how you want the subscribe option to appear (icon, button, or inline form)
3. Customize the description text
4. Set up automatic integration with your email service

**Tip**: Link expiration is perfect for limited-time offers, presale announcements, or time-sensitive promotions. QR codes make it easy for fans to access your link page from printed materials.

## Best Practices

- **Regular Updates**: Keep your link page fresh by updating links when you release new music or content
- **Strategic Ordering**: Place your most important links at the top where fans will see them first
- **Cross-Promotion**: Use social links to connect all your platforms and create a complete fan experience
- **Analytics Review**: Check your analytics regularly to understand what content performs best
- **Mobile Optimization**: Test your link page on mobile devices since most fans access it from phones

These advanced features help you create a professional, engaging link page that converts fans into loyal supporters of your music career.
