# Getting Started on the Artist Platform

Welcome to the Extra Chill Artist Platform! This guide will walk you through joining as an artist and setting up your artist profile and link page.

## What is the Artist Platform?

The Artist Platform is your home base on Extra Chill. It lets you:

- **Create an Artist Profile** - Share your music, bio, and genre information with the community
- **Build a Link Page** - A custom landing page (at `extrachill.link/your-name`) where fans can find all your music, social links, and more
- **Collect Subscribers** - Gather email addresses from fans interested in your work
- **Track Analytics** - See how many people visit your link page and which links get the most clicks
- **Invite Band Members** - Add collaborators to manage your artist presence

---

## Joining as an Artist

### Step 1: Start the Join Flow

The easiest way to join the Artist Platform is through our dedicated join page:

1. Visit **[extrachill.link/join](https://extrachill.link/join)**
2. You'll see options to either:
   - **Create a New Account** - If you're new to Extra Chill
   - **Log In to Existing Account** - If you already have an Extra Chill community account

### Step 2: Complete Registration (New Users Only)

If you're creating a new account:

1. Fill in your **Username** and **Email Address**
2. Create a **Password** (keep it secure!)
3. Click **Register**
4. You'll be automatically redirected to customize your artist profile

### Step 3: Existing Users

If you already have an Extra Chill account and want to add an artist profile:

1. Log in with your existing credentials
2. You can create a new artist profile from your dashboard
3. Skip ahead to [Setting Up Your Artist Profile](#setting-up-your-artist-profile)

## Setting Up Your Artist Profile

Once registered, you'll see your Artist Profile setup. Here's what to fill in:

### Basic Information

**Artist/Band Name**
- The name that will appear on your profile and link page
- This becomes your URL slug (for example: `extrachill.link/your-artist-name`)

**Biography**
- A brief description of your music and artistic style
- Tell fans what kind of music you make and why they should follow you
- This appears on your public profile page

**Profile Image**
- Upload a high-quality photo or logo representing your artist brand
- This image appears on your profile, in artist directories, and on your link page
- Recommended size: 500x500 pixels

### Genre and Location

- Select your primary **Genre** from the available options
- Add your **Location** (city/state or country)
- These help fans discover you in the artist directory

### Social Links

Add links to your music and social media accounts:

- **Apple Music**, **Spotify**, **Bandcamp**, **SoundCloud** - Music streaming platforms
- **Instagram**, **TikTok**, **Twitter/X**, **YouTube** - Social media channels
- **Patreon**, **Venmo** - Support and fan funding platforms
- **Custom Links** - Add any other URL you want to share

**Tips for Social Links:**
- Include all major platforms where your music is available
- Keep links current and working
- You can add or change these anytime

### Save Your Profile

After filling in your information, click **Save** or **Update Profile**. Your artist profile is now live!

## Creating Your Link Page

Your link page is the centerpiece of your Artist Platform presence. It's a beautiful, customizable landing page accessible at **extrachill.link/your-artist-name**.

### What is a Link Page?

Think of it as your digital hub. Your link page includes:

- Your artist name and bio
- Links to all your music platforms
- Subscription form to collect fan emails
- Custom colors and styling to match your brand
- Analytics showing visitor activity

### Setting Up Your Link Page

1. **Go to Your Link Page Manager**
   - Visit **artist.extrachill.com** and look for "Manage Link Pages"
   - Or navigate to your dashboard after registration

2. **Link Page Editor**
   - You'll see a tabbed interface with different sections:
     - **Info** - Artist information and profile details
     - **Links** - Add and organize your links
     - **Customize** - Colors, fonts, backgrounds, and styling
     - **Advanced** - Analytics tracking, link expiration, and more
     - **Socials** - Social media platform links

### Building Your Links

The **Links** tab is where you organize the destinations you want fans to visit:

**Adding a Link:**
1. Click **Add New Link**
2. Enter the **Link Text** (what it says on your page, e.g., "Stream on Spotify")
3. Enter the **URL** (the full link, starting with `https://`)
4. Click **Save**

**Organizing Your Links:**
- **Drag and Drop** - Click and hold a link to reorder it
- **Group into Sections** - Organize related links together (e.g., "Music Platforms", "Support Me")
- **Delete Links** - Remove any link you no longer want

**Link Types You Might Include:**
- Streaming platforms (Spotify, Apple Music, YouTube Music)
- Downloads (Bandcamp, your own store)
- Social media (Instagram, TikTok, Twitter)
- Support (Patreon, Venmo, PayPal)
- Website or merchandise store
- Concert/event links

### Customizing Your Page Design

The **Customize** tab lets you make your link page look uniquely yours:

**Colors**
- Set your **Primary Color** (highlights and main accent)
- Set your **Secondary Color** (additional accent)
- Choose a **Background Color** or pattern

**Fonts**
- Select **Font Style** that matches your brand
- Choose from pre-selected font families that work well on web

**Images and Branding**
- Upload a **Header Image** (banner at the top)
- Choose your **Profile Image** placement
- Add any custom background

**Button Styling**
- Customize link button colors and rounded corners
- Make your page feel polished and professional

### Advanced Settings

The **Advanced** tab includes powerful features for tracking and optimization:

**Analytics Tracking**
- Enable **Google Analytics** integration to track visitor behavior
- Enable **Meta Pixel (Facebook)** to track conversions
- These help you understand your audience

**Link Expiration**
- Set links to **automatically activate/deactivate** on specific dates
- Useful for limited-time offers or tour dates
- Schedule promotions in advance

**YouTube Embed Control**
- Choose to embed videos directly on your link page
- Great for displaying recent music videos or performances

**Subscription Settings**
- Customize your **Email Subscription Form**
- Choose to collect subscriber information inline or in a popup

### Viewing Your Live Link Page

Once you've set up your links and customization:

1. Click **View Page** or visit **extrachill.link/your-artist-name** directly
2. You'll see your public link page exactly as fans see it
3. Your page is live immediately‚Äîno approval needed!

## Understanding Your Analytics

Your link page includes built-in analytics to track visitor engagement.

### Key Metrics

**Page Views**
- Total number of times people visited your link page
- Helps you see if your promotion efforts are working

**Link Clicks**
- Which links your fans are clicking on most
- Shows you which platforms matter most to your audience

**Visit Trends**
- See patterns of traffic over time
- Identify when your audience is most active

### Using Analytics

1. Go to your **Link Page Manager**
2. Select the **Analytics** section
3. View charts showing:
   - Daily page views
   - Link click breakdown
   - Top-performing links

### Tips for Growing Your Traffic

- Share your link page on social media: **extrachill.link/your-artist-name**
- Include it in your bio on Instagram, TikTok, and other platforms
- Add it to your email signature
- Share it in Discord, Reddit, or music communities
- Print it on merchandise or business cards

## Managing Your Artist Profile as a Band

If you're part of a band or collaborative project, you can invite other members to help manage your artist profile.

### Inviting Band Members

1. Go to your **Artist Profile Manager**
2. Select the **Members** or **Roster** tab
3. Click **Invite Member**
4. Enter the **email address** of the band member
5. They'll receive an email invitation to join
6. Once they accept, they can edit the profile alongside you

### Member Permissions

Band members can:
- Edit artist profile information
- Add and organize links
- Customize the look of your page
- View analytics
- Manage subscribers

---

## Collecting Fan Emails

Your link page can include a subscription form to collect fan email addresses.

### Enabling Email Collection

1. Go to your **Link Page Manager**
2. In the **Advanced** tab, find **Subscription Settings**
3. Enable **Email Subscription Form**
4. Customize the form message (e.g., "Subscribe for tour updates")

### Where the Form Appears

- As a **popup modal** when fans first visit your link page
- Or **inline** at the bottom of your page (depends on your settings)

### Viewing Your Subscribers

1. Go to your **Artist Profile Manager**
2. Select the **Subscribers** tab
3. You'll see a list of all email addresses collected
4. You can **Export** your subscriber list as a CSV file

### Using Your Subscriber List

- Use a service like **Sendy**, **Mailchimp**, or **Substack** to send updates
- Share new music releases with your fans
- Announce tour dates and events
- Keep your audience engaged between releases

## Troubleshooting

### I can't log in

1. Make sure you're using the correct email address
2. Try the **Password Reset** option on the login page
3. If you registered through join flow, you should have received a confirmation email

### My link page isn't showing my updates

Updates should appear immediately. If they don't:
1. Try refreshing the page (press **Ctrl+R** or **Cmd+R**)
2. Clear your browser cache
3. Try a different browser or device

### A link isn't working

1. Copy the link and test it directly in your browser
2. Make sure it starts with `https://`
3. If it's a social media link, verify the URL is still active
4. Update or delete broken links from your manager

### I forgot my password

1. Visit the **login page** at **artist.extrachill.com**
2. Click **Forgot Password**
3. Enter your email address
4. Follow the reset instructions in your email

### I want to delete my link page or artist profile

Contact our support team‚Äîwe can help you remove your profile or archive your data.
