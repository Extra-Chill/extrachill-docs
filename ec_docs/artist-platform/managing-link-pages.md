# Managing Your Link Page

Customize your extrachill.link page to showcase your music and connect with fans.

## Accessing the Editor

1. Log in to [artist.extrachill.com](https://artist.extrachill.com)
2. Navigate to your link page management
3. Use the visual editor to make changes

## Adding and Organizing Links

### Adding Links

- Click "Add Link" in the editor
- Enter the link title and URL
- Choose an icon or upload a custom image
- Set link options like expiration dates

### Organizing Links

- Drag and drop links to reorder them
- Group related links together
- Use sections to organize different types of content

## Customizing Appearance

### Design Options

- **Colors**: Choose theme colors that match your brand
- **Fonts**: Select from available font options
- **Background**: Upload custom background images or use solid colors

### Branding

- Add your artist logo or profile image
- Include a custom bio or description
- Set up social media links in the header

## Advanced Features

### Link Expiration

Set links to automatically deactivate after a certain date, perfect for time-limited promotions.

### Click Tracking

Monitor which links get the most clicks to understand what content resonates with your audience.

### Subscriber Collection

Add email collection forms to build your mailing list directly from your link page.

## Publishing Changes

- Preview your changes before publishing
- Save drafts to work on updates over time
- Publish when ready to make changes live

## Best Practices

- Keep your most important links at the top
- Use clear, descriptive link titles
- Regularly update your content to keep fans engaged
- Test all links to ensure they work correctly

## Troubleshooting

### Changes Not Appearing

- Clear your browser cache
- Check that changes are published, not just saved as draft
- Wait a few minutes for updates to propagate

### Links Not Working

- Verify URLs are correct and include http:// or https://
- Test links in an incognito/private browsing window
- Contact support if issues persist
