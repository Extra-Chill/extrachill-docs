# Managing Your Artist Profile

The artist profile manager is your central hub for updating your artist information, adding team members, and managing your email subscribers. Access this tool from artist.extrachill.com/manage-artist-profiles/ when logged in.

## Accessing the Profile Manager

Navigate to artist.extrachill.com/manage-artist-profiles/ while logged in. If you have access to multiple artist profiles, you will see a dropdown at the top of the page to switch between them.

## Info Tab

The Info tab contains your core artist profile information.

**Profile Picture**
- Upload a profile image that appears on your artist profile
- Click the file input to select an image from your device
- Use the Remove button to delete the current image
- The image appears as a preview when uploaded

**Header Image**
- Upload a banner image that appears at the top of your profile
- Click the file input to select an image from your device
- Use the Remove button to delete the current image
- The image appears as a preview when uploaded

**Artist Name**
- Enter your artist name or band name (required field)
- This is the primary identifier for your profile
- Must be saved for changes to take effect

**City / Region**
- Enter your location, such as your city or region
- Optional field for your geographic information

**Genre**
- Enter your music genre or style
- Optional field for categorization

**Artist Bio**
- Write a biography describing yourself or your music
- Use the text area to add detailed information
- Changes must be saved to persist

## Profile Managers Tab

The Profile Managers tab allows you to invite team members to help manage your artist profile.

**Inviting Team Members**
- Type an email address into the search field
- The system will search for existing Extra Chill users as you type
- Click on a user from the dropdown to select them, or enter a valid email
- Click Send Invite to send an invitation
- Pending invitations appear in the list with a Pending status

**Removing Team Members**
- Find the team member in the list
- Click the Remove button next to their name
- The member will no longer have access to manage your profile

**Current Team Members**
- Active team members display with their display name
- If they have an Extra Chill profile, their name links to their profile
- Some members may have status indicators
- Use the Remove button to revoke access at any time

## Subscribers Tab

The Subscribers tab displays email addresses collected from your link page.

**Viewing Subscribers**
- Subscribers appear in a list with their email address
- If a subscriber has an Extra Chill username, it appears as a tag
- The date they subscribed appears as a tag
- Twenty subscribers display per page

**Pagination**
- Use Prev and Next buttons to navigate through pages
- The current page and total pages display between navigation buttons

**Exporting Subscribers**
- Click the Export CSV button to download your subscriber list
- This exports only new subscribers (those not previously exported)
- Use this to import subscribers into your email marketing service
- The button is disabled if you have no subscribers

## Artist Context Switching

If you have access to multiple artist profiles, use the dropdown in the header to switch between them. The page content updates automatically when you select a different artist.

## Saving Changes

Always click the Save button in the header when you make changes on the Info tab. A Saved message confirms successful updates. Changes to team members and subscribers save automatically.

## Viewing Your Profile

Click the View Profile button in the header to see how your artist profile appears publicly.
